Regulatory Framework & Legal Compliance

arkly.capital operates under strict protocols, ensuring our platform meets the highest standards for security, transparency, and user protection.

Legal Framework

Our legal framework is designed to comply with securities regulations across multiple jurisdictions, providing enterprise-grade compliance for our tokenized real estate offerings.

SEC Compliance

All token offerings comply with U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission regulations, including Regulation D and Regulation S exemptions.

Multi-Jurisdiction

Structured to comply with regulations in the EU (MiFID II), UK (FCA), and other major financial jurisdictions.

AML/KYC

Robust Anti-Money Laundering and Know Your Customer procedures ensure compliance with FATF guidelines.

Token Legal Framework

All arkly tokens are structured as security tokens under applicable securities laws, providing investors with legally enforceable rights to underlying real estate assets and income distributions.

Security Token Status

Tokens represent fractional ownership interests in specific real estate assets, structured as securities under U.S. federal securities laws.

Investor Rights

Token holders receive proportional rights to rental income, appreciation, and voting on major asset decisions.

Transfer Restrictions

Secondary transfers subject to compliance with applicable securities laws, including holding periods and accredited investor requirements.
