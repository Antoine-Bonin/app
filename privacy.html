Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your personal information

Last updated: December 2024

1. Our Decentralized Approach

No Personal Data Collection

As a truly decentralized platform, arkly.capital operates without collecting personal information. We embrace the principles of:

  • Pseudonymous interactions through wallet addresses only
  • No KYC or identity verification requirements
  • No storage of personal data on our servers
  • Complete user sovereignty over their own data
  • Self-custody of assets without intermediary control

Technical Data Only

The only information we process relates to blockchain transactions and technical operations:

  • Wallet addresses (public blockchain data)
  • Transaction hashes and smart contract interactions
  • Basic website analytics (anonymized)
  • Technical performance metrics

2. How We Operate

Our decentralized architecture ensures:

  • Blockchain Transactions: All asset ownership and transfers are recorded on public blockchains
  • Smart Contract Automation: Property management and yield distribution through code, not intermediaries
  • User Sovereignty: You maintain complete control over your private keys and assets
  • Transparency: All transactions and protocol operations are publicly verifiable
  • Censorship Resistance: No central authority can freeze or confiscate your assets

3. No Data Sharing

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to share. The platform operates through:

  • Smart Contracts: Automated code execution without human intervention
  • Blockchain Networks: Public, decentralized infrastructure
  • IPFS Storage: Distributed file storage for property documents
  • No Third Parties: No intermediaries with access to your information

Your privacy is protected by design, not by policy.

4. Blockchain Transparency

Important considerations for blockchain interactions:

  • All transactions are recorded on public blockchains
  • Wallet addresses are pseudonymous but publicly visible
  • Smart contract interactions are transparent and verifiable
  • Property ownership records are immutable and public
  • You control your own privacy through wallet management

5. Technical Security

Our platform implements robust security through:

  • Smart contract audits by leading security firms
  • Multi-signature wallet requirements for protocol management
  • Decentralized infrastructure without single points of failure
  • Open-source code for community verification
  • Regular security updates and bug bounty programs

6. No Data Retention

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no retention policy needed. The blockchain itself maintains:

  • Property ownership records (immutable)
  • Transaction history (permanent)
  • Smart contract state (distributed)
  • Public property documents (IPFS)

7. Age Requirements

Users must be at least 18 years old to understand:

  • The risks of self-custody and private key management
  • Investment risks in tokenized real estate
  • Blockchain transaction finality
  • Personal responsibility for regulatory compliance in their jurisdiction

8. Protocol Evolution

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Announcing changes through our official social media channels
  • Displaying prominent notices on our platform
