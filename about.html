Our Story

Building the Future

Born from frustration with closed real estate markets, Arkly was created to bring borderless access, trustless infrastructure, and real yield to premium assets.

Since late 2024, Arkly has enabled Solana-native exposure to tokenized real estate without KYC, intermediaries, or friction.
